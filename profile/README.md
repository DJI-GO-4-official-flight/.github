## What is DJI GO 4

**DJI GO 4** is the official mobile application for controlling **DJI Phantom 4, Mavic Pro, Inspire 2, Spark**, and other drones.  
It provides full access to flight telemetry, live HD video streaming, intelligent flight modes, and camera settings.  
The app turns your smartphone or tablet into a professional cockpit and media station â€” combining control, filming, analytics, and cloud sync in one interface.

## Development and evolution

DJI GO 4 was designed alongside the new Mavic platform and re-engineered from the original GO app.  
Over the years, it evolved to include HDR video processing, real-time streaming, AI flight analytics, and DJI Cloud integration.  
Today, it is widely used in professional filmmaking, mapping, construction, agriculture, and inspection.
